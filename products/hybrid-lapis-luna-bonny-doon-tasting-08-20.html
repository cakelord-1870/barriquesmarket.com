Hybrid Lapis Luna - Bonny Doon Tasting 08/20

Regular price
Sold out
Sale price
$15.00 Sale
Quantity must be 1 or more

This tasting is no longer available to purchase online.

 Whether you are ready to taste in person or need to continue experiencing our tastings virtually, our new hybrid tasting format could be just what you are looking for.  On Friday, August 20th @ 7pm at Barriques Monroe St, we will be hosting a tasting featuring wines from Lapis Luna and the iconic Bonny Doon with our guest host, Andrew Nelson, who will be joining us virtually.  You can taste along with us & Andrew in person at Monroe St. or tune into the livestream of the event via Zoom.  The in-person tasting will feature 6 wines including the 2019 Lapis Luna Sauvignon Blanc, 2019 Lapis Luna Red, 2019 Lapis Luna Zinfandel, 2019 Lapis Luna Napa Cabernet, 2019 Bonny Doon Picpoul and the 2020 Bonny Doon Van Gris de Cigare.

For this attending virtually, we have a four wine option (featuring the Lapis Luna wines) and a six wine option featuring all the wines we will discuss that evening.  Both of these options include 8oz pours of the respective wines.  Virtual attendees will receive an email with instructions on how to connect with us via Zoom the night of the event.  If you select the in-person version, we will open the doors at Monroe St. at 6:45pm on the 20th.
